About AAV Works Pvt. Ltd.

AAV Works Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized organization dedicated to **Railway Signaling & Telecommunication (S&T) Design & Drawings**. Established on **07-03-2023**, we focus on preparing **Railway Signaling Drawings** in compliance with Indian Railways' standard practices.

Located in **Hyderabad, Telangana**, with a **Registered Office in Chirala, Andhra Pradesh**, we are a team of **highly experienced Railway Experts**, including **retired professionals from the Indian Railways' S&T Department**. Our expertise covers various **signaling design aspects**, including **SIP, TOC, RCC, and advanced signaling techniques**.

Our mission is to enhance the **quality of Railway S&T Design & Drawings** by adopting **the latest guidelines issued by Indian Railways**. We aim to set new standards in the industry by providing **precise, reliable, and innovative solutions** for railway operations.
